Echoes of an Unsaid Goodbye!

—Misery of a Man — The Tales—

Author’s Note

This work is a piece of fiction. All characters, events, names, and dialogues are products of imagination.

Any resemblance to real persons, living or dead, or to actual events is purely coincidental.

The emotions portrayed are real — inspired by universal experiences of love, loss, and human connection.

Written by Likith Naidu, B.Tech student from India. This book marks his first attempt to give voice to silence.
